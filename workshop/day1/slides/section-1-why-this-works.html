What Actually Works

× What I tried first
  • Pasting requirements into chat and hoping for the best
  • Building full features in one shot
  • Not reviewing AI output carefully enough
! The turning point
  • Treating AI like a junior developer, not a magic box
  • Realizing the quality of input determines the quality of output
✓ What works now
  • Detailed specs before touching AI tools
  • Small, testable chunks
  • Test-first, then implement
  • Review like a PR

The Bottleneck Is You,
Not the AI

The AI can write code. That's solved.
What it can't do: read your mind.
The bottleneck is the quality of your instructions.
Better instructions = better output. Every time.
